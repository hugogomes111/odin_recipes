Lasagna

Image of a hoemade cooked lasagna

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of perfectly cooked lasagna noodles, a rich and savory meat sauce made from ground beef or Italian sausage, and a creamy ricotta cheese mixture. The meat sauce is infused with garlic, onions, and Italian herbs, simmered to enhance the flavors. Between the layers of pasta, there's a smooth ricotta filling mixed with egg and parsley, adding a delightful richness. Topping each layer is a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese that melts beautifully during baking. Once baked, the lasagna forms a golden, bubbly top and a hearty, flavorful interior, with all the layers coming together in perfect harmony. It's a comforting, satisfying dish that’s great for family dinners or gatherings.

List of ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles- 12 sheets (regular or no-boil)
  • Ground beef– 1 lb (or Italian sausage)
  • Marinara sauce– 3 cups (or homemade tomato sauce)
  • Garlic- 2 cloves, minced
  • Onion- 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Italian seasoning- 1-2 tsp
  • Ricotta cheese- 15 oz
  • Egg- 1 large
  • Fresh parsley- 2 tbsp, chopped
  • shredded mozzarella cheese- 2-3 cups
  • Grated Parmesan cheese- 1 cup
  • Olive oil- for sautéing
  • Salt and pepper- to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the meat.
  2. Add pasta sauce to the meat
  3. Boil the lasagna noodles
  4. Mix ricotta cheese
  5. Layer sauce, noodles, cheese in a baking dish
  6. Repeat the layers
  7. Add cheese on top
  8. Bake the lasagna
